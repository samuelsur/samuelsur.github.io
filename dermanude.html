DermaNude dataset

This is a dataset of skin which is suitable for the tasks of skin lesion matching and longitudinal trackin. It has been created as a part of bachelor's thesis Detection, classification and matching of skin lesions and has been expanded during the master's thesis Longitudinal Matching of Skin Lesions

Specs

  • Cameras used: Nikon D3400, Realme 8, Iphone 12, Iphone 15
  • The DermaNudeV1 and DermaNudeV2 images are ~2 years apart
  • The datasets are annotated with bounding boxes around skin lesions
